About Us

Meet the Owner

Tysen Gale has been working in professional landscaping with his family since 2010. He has a deep knowledge in the field and will always make sure the job is done right. He has been married for over a year and a half and loves his family. He also enjoys football, trucks, and exercising.

Our Business

At Tysen Gale Landscaping, we believe every property deserves to be a place you can take pride in. As a family-owned business, we value honesty, hard work, and treating every customer like a neighbor. Although we're new to Eastern Idaho, our experienced landscaping team brings years of knowledge and a commitment to delivering quality results on every project. We are proud to serve homeowners and businesses throughout Rexburg, Sugar City, Anthony, Driggs, and Rigby with dependable lawn care, landscaping, and irrigation services. Whether we're maintaining a lawn, designing a new landscape, or installing an efficient irrigation system, our goal is to provide workmanship that lasts and service you can trust. Family is at the heart of everything we do, and we look forward to helping families across Eastern Idaho create outdoor spaces they can enjoy for years to come.
